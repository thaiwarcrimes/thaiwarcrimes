## Morning Attacks in Military Region 5 (12 December 2025)

On the morning of **12 December 2025**, Thai military forces intensified attacks against Cambodian positions and villages in **Military Region 5**.

- **6:27 AM**: Artillery shelling targeted **Tumnob Dach Village**  
- **6:40 AM**: Thai **infantry entered Chok Chey Village**  
- **7:02 AM**: Infantry entered **Prey Chan Village** and assaulted **Chok Chey Village**  
- **7:10 AM – 7:20 AM**: Shelling intensified in **Prey Chan Village**  
- **7:43 AM**: **Armored vehicles** entered **Boeung Trakoun**  
- **7:57 AM**: Shelling followed in **Boeung Trakoun**

## Sustained Attacks in the 4th Military Zone (7:05 AM – 1:00 PM)

In the **4th Military Zone**, Thai attacks continued throughout the morning:

- **7:40 AM – 9:00 AM**: Attacks targeted **Chub Angkunh**, **Thmor Chat**, **Bos Sbov Cheam Tea**, **An Ses**, **Thmor Daun**, **Phnom 333**, **Ta Krabei**, and **Ta Moan**
- **8:20 AM**: **Tanks and infantry** attacked **Thmor Chat** and **Ta Krabei Temples**
- **8:41 AM**: Attacks targeted **Bos Sbov Cheam Tea**
- **10:12 AM**: **Poisonous smoke drones** deployed in **Phnom Khmoch** and **Bos Sbov Cheam Tea**
- **11:00 AM**: Shelling intensified in **An Ses**, accompanied by **poisonous smoke**
- **12:00 PM**: Additional strikes targeted:
  - Ta Sim Koul 8  
  - Phnom Khmoch  
  - Cham Te  
  - Ta Krabei  
  - Phnom 333  
  - Chub Angkunh  
  - Thmor Daun  
  - Damnak King  
  - Ta Moan  
  - Chub Koki
- **12:32 PM**: **Bomb-equipped drones** deployed near **Preah Vihear Temple**

## Continued Attacks in Military Region 5 (8:38 AM – Evening)

Thai attacks in **Military Region 5** continued throughout the day:

- **8:38 AM**: Infantry with **tanks entered Prey Chan Village**
- **9:10 AM**: **Reconnaissance drones** monitored **Prey Chan**
- **10:26 AM**: **Bomb-equipped drones** targeted **O Phluok Damrey**
- **10:34 AM**: Reconnaissance drones continued monitoring **Prey Chan**
- **11:37 AM**: **Toxic gas** deployed in **Prey Chan Village**
- **11:58 AM**: Bomb-equipped drones attacked **O Phluok Damrey**
- **12:13 PM**: **Toxic gas** deployed in **Prey Chan** and **Chok Chey Villages**
- **12:45 PM**: Reconnaissance drones monitored **Prey Chan**
- **12:53 PM**: **Artillery shells** fired into **Prey Chan Village**
- **3:19 PM**: **Drone bombing** occurred in **Prey Chan Village**
- **3:51 PM**: Infantry with **tanks attempted to invade Chok Chey Village**
- **5:14 PM & 5:53 PM**: Reconnaissance drones monitored areas behind **Prey Chan** and **Chok Chey Villages**
- **5:54 PM**: **Drone bombing** in **Prey Chan Village** continued

## Cambodia’s Official Position

The Royal Government of Cambodia:
- Strongly condemns the continued **ground incursions, drone bombings, artillery attacks, and chemical deployments**
- Reaffirms that all affected areas lie within **Cambodian sovereign territory**
- Denounces attacks on **villages, temples, and civilian-adjacent areas**
- Calls for immediate cessation of hostilities and accountability under international law  

## Conclusion

The events of **12 December 2025** represent a sustained and coordinated escalation by Thai military forces across Cambodia’s Military Regions 4 and 5. The use of infantry incursions, armored vehicles, artillery, bomb-equipped drones, and poisonous smoke against villages and sacred sites constituted grave violations of Cambodia’s sovereignty and international humanitarian norms, while Cambodian forces continued to defend their territory under a disciplined and defensive posture.
