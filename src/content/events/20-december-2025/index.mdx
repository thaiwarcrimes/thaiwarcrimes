## Overview

The Ministry of National Defence of Cambodia confirms that from **19 to 20 December 2025**, Thai military forces continued their armed aggression against Cambodian sovereign territory across the **3rd, 4th, and 5th Military Regions**. These actions demonstrate a clear escalation of hostilities through coordinated **naval, air, and ground operations**, directly targeting civilian areas, cultural and religious sites, educational institutions, and commercial infrastructure.

## 3rd Military Region – Frontline Situation  
*(Koh Kong Province | 06:30 PM, 19 Dec – 05:00 PM, 20 Dec 2025)*

- **06:30 PM – 06:55 PM (19 Dec)**: Thai naval warship launched approximately **20 artillery shells**, falling into the water behind a hotel near the **Cham Yeam Border Checkpoint**, Mondul Seima District  
- The situation later returned to calm; Cambodian forces continued monitoring with high alertness

## 4th Military Region – Frontline Situation  
*(Oddar Meanchey and Preah Vihear Provinces | 05:00 PM, 19 Dec – 05:00 PM, 20 Dec 2025)*

### 19 December 2025
- **07:30 PM**: Thai forces used **heavy artillery and tanks**, supported by infantry, attempting to infiltrate **Phnom Khmoch** and **Bos Sbov – Choam Te**
- **10:58 PM**: Thai Air Force deployed **F-16 fighter jets**, dropping **two bombs** to destroy **O’ Jik Bridge**, a civilian infrastructure near populated areas at the border between **Chong Kal District (Oddar Meanchey)** and **Srei Snam District (Siem Reap)**

### 20 December 2025
- **04:00 AM – 05:30 AM**: Continuous shelling targeted **Preah Vihear Temple**
- **04:00 AM**: Shelling also targeted **Thmar Daun**
- **As of 03:00 PM**: Thai forces continued attacks using **artillery and tank-mounted guns** in **Phnom Khmoch** and **Choam Te**
- **04:00 PM**: Thai forces used **DK (106) guns and artillery** to fire into **Preah Vihear Temple**

The situation in other areas remained calm; Cambodian forces maintained heightened vigilance.

## 5th Military Region – Frontline Situation  
*(Banteay Meanchey and Pursat Provinces | 05:00 PM, 19 Dec – 05:00 PM, 20 Dec 2025)*

### 19 December 2025
- **07:10 PM**: Thai forces fired **flare rounds** in **Prey Chan Village**
- **07:50 PM**: Shelling targeted **Chok Chey Village**

### 20 December 2025
- **12:04 AM**: Shelling targeted **Boeung Trakoun**
- **04:00 AM – 04:02 AM**: Artillery and tank shells continued to strike **Boeung Trakoun**
- **05:48 AM – 06:35 AM**: Thai forces fired **75mm tank-mounted guns** into **O’ Phlok Damrei**
- **08:13 AM**: Shelling targeted **Prey Chan Village**
- **09:20 AM**: Reconnaissance drones violated Cambodian airspace over **Veal Veng District**, advancing toward **Phnom 1500**
- **09:30 AM – 11:30 AM**: Sustained artillery fire into **Boeung Trakoun**
- **10:30 AM**: Reconnaissance drones violated airspace over **Thmar Da Commune**
- **11:01 AM – 11:16 AM**: Thai Air Force deployed **F-16 fighter jets**, dropping:
  - **3 bombs** on a civilian commercial building near **Chey Chomneas Border Checkpoint**
  - **2 bombs** on a **primary school** in **Thmar Da Commune**, Veal Veng District
- **11:15 AM**: Additional reconnaissance drones violated Cambodian airspace over **Thmar Da**
- **11:46 AM**: Thai infantry, supported by approximately **5 armored vehicles**, invaded Cambodian territory near **Chey Chomneas Border Checkpoint**
- **12:38 PM**: Shelling targeted **Boeung Trakoun**
- **01:04 PM**: Shelling targeted **Prey Chan Village**
- **01:43 PM**: Continued shelling in **Boeung Trakoun**
- **02:10 PM**: Drone bombardment targeted **Chey Chomneas Border Checkpoint**
- **02:15 PM**: Thai forces fired **two 155mm artillery shells** into a densely populated civilian area in **Svay Chek District**, Banteay Meanchey Province
- **03:42 PM**: Reconnaissance drones violated airspace behind **Chok Chey Village**
- **04:00 PM**: Reconnaissance drones violated airspace behind **Boeung Trakoun**
- **04:11 PM**: Continued shelling targeted **Boeung Trakoun**

## Ongoing Developments (20 December 2025 Evening)

- **05:30 PM**: Thai forces continued firing **heavy and supporting artillery** into **Thmar Daun** (4th Military Region)

## Conclusion

The events of **19–20 December 2025** demonstrate sustained and coordinated Thai military aggression across multiple Cambodian provinces, involving **naval shelling, airstrikes, ground incursions, and drone operations**. Cambodian forces continue to **repel and resist** the invasion while remaining fully committed to defending Cambodia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity, and civilian population.

Further updates will be provided as the situation develops.
