## Timeline of Incidents (14 December 2025 | 08:00 AM – 01:00 PM)

- **08:15 AM**: Thai **F-16 fighter jet** dropped **one bomb** in the **C1 area**, **Ek Pheap Village**, Thmar Da Commune, Veal Veng District, **Pursat Province**
- **09:52 AM**: Thai forces shelled **Prey Chan Village**
- **10:36 AM**: Thai **F-16 fighter jets** dropped **two bombs** in **Thmar Sen Village**, Nimitt Commune, **O’ Chrov District**, Banteay Meanchey Province
- **10:38 AM**: F-16 fighter jets flew over **O’ Bei Choan Commune**, violating Cambodian airspace
- **10:58 AM**: **Reconnaissance drones** monitored **O’ Bei Choan Commune**
- **11:20 AM**: Thai **infantry forces attempted to infiltrate** the area in front of **Boeung Trakoun**
- **11:55 AM**: Continued artillery shelling targeted **Prey Chan Village**
- **12:25 PM**: Thai forces shelled **Boeung Trakoun**

## Situation Assessment

During the reporting period, Thai military actions demonstrated a coordinated pattern of escalation involving:
- Airstrikes by **F-16 fighter jets**
- Artillery shelling of **civilian villages**
- Drone-based reconnaissance
- Attempted ground infiltration by infantry forces

All incidents occurred within **Cambodian sovereign territory**.

## Cambodia’s Official Position

The Royal Government of Cambodia:
- Strongly condemns Thailand’s continued armed aggression  
- Reaffirms Cambodia’s **sovereignty and territorial integrity**  
- Denounces attacks on **civilian villages and infrastructure**  
- Confirms that further detailed information will be released as the situation develops  

## Conclusion

The events of **14 December 2025** reflect continued Thai military aggression in Cambodia’s **5th Military Region**, marked by airstrikes, artillery shelling, drone operations, and attempted ground incursions. These actions constitute serious violations of Cambodia’s sovereignty and international humanitarian norms, while Cambodian forces remained vigilant and defensive in protecting national territory.
