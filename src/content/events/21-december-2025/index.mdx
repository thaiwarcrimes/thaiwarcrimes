## Overview

The Ministry of National Defence of Cambodia confirms that on **21 December 2025**, Thai military forces continued their armed aggression against Cambodia, entering the **15th consecutive day** of hostilities. These actions represent ongoing violations of the ceasefire and the **Joint Declaration signed by the Prime Ministers of Cambodia and Thailand on 26 October 2025**.

Thai forces employed **heavy artillery, tanks, mortars, fighter jets, drones, and toxic gas**, deliberately targeting **civilian communities, religious and cultural heritage sites, schools, and civilian infrastructure**, constituting serious violations of international humanitarian law.

## 4th Military Region – Frontline Situation  
*(Oddar Meanchey and Preah Vihear Provinces | 05:00 PM, 20 Dec – 10:00 AM, 21 Dec 2025)*

### 20 December 2025
- **05:30 PM**: Thai forces fired **heavy and supporting artillery** into the area of **Thmar Daun**

### 21 December 2025
- **12:05 AM**: Thai forces fired **three mortar rounds** into **Tamone Temple**
- **06:15 AM**: Heavy artillery and **tank-mounted fire** targeted **Thmar Daun** and **Tamone Temple**
- **06:25 AM**: **Heavy and supporting artillery**, supported by **drones**, struck **Tamone Temple**
- **09:31 AM**: **Toxic gas** deployed into the area of **Preah Vihear Temple**

The situation in other areas remained calm; Cambodian forces continued close monitoring with high vigilance.

## 5th Military Region – Frontline Situation  
*(Banteay Meanchey Province | 12:00 AM – 05:00 PM, 21 Dec 2025)*

### Morning Attacks
- **05:50 AM**: Artillery shelling targeted **Boeung Trakoun**
- **06:09 AM**: **Reconnaissance drones** violated Cambodian airspace over **Prey Chan Village**
- **07:09 AM**: Shelling targeted **Prey Chan Village**
- **09:27 AM**: Continued shelling targeted **Prey Chan Village**

### Escalation Against Civilian Areas
- **01:01 PM**: Fighter jets dropped **two bombs** into **Phkoam (Pkam) Commune**, **Svay Chek District**
- **01:35 PM**: **Nine artillery shells** struck civilian areas in **Nimit Commune**, **O’ Chrov District**
- **02:35 PM – 02:57 PM**: **Eleven artillery shells** targeted **Poipet District**, **Poipet Municipality**
- **02:10 PM**: **Drone bombardment** near **Chey Chomneas Border Checkpoint**
- **03:42 PM**: **Reconnaissance drones** violated airspace behind **Chok Chey Village**
- **04:00 PM**: **Reconnaissance drones** violated airspace behind **Boeung Trakoun**
- **04:11 PM**: Continued shelling targeted **Boeung Trakoun**

## Civilian Impact and Casualties

Cambodia reiterates that its right to self-defence is exercised solely against military targets. Conversely, Thai forces deliberately attacked civilian locations.

As of **6:00 PM on 20 December 2025**:
- **19 civilians killed**, including **1 infant**
- **79 civilians injured**

## Legal Assessment and Official Position

Thailand’s actions—including the use of **fighter jets, heavy artillery, drones, and toxic gas**—constitute **serious violations of international humanitarian law**, the **United Nations Charter**, and the **ASEAN Charter**.

Cambodia demands:
- Immediate cessation of hostilities
- Full reimplementation of the **Joint Declaration**
- Accountability for violations and war crimes

## Conclusion

The events of **21 December 2025** confirm continued Thai armed aggression on the **15th consecutive day**. Cambodian Armed Forces and National Police remain **steadfast** in defending Cambodia’s **sovereignty, territorial integrity, and civilian population**.

Further updates will be provided as the situation develops.
