## Overview

On December 24, 2025, at approximately 10 a.m., Thai military forces conducted an airstrike using a T-50 fighter jet to drop bombs near Phnom Sampov in Battambang province, Cambodia. The attack caused widespread panic among local residents, particularly schoolchildren attending a nearby primary school, who were forced to flee in terror.
